# CELSUX 360 — Brand Identity

**Company:** All-in-one corporate events agency. Buenos Aires, Argentina. 25 years, 3,000+ events. Clients: DirecTV, Adidas, McDonald's, LATAM, BBVA, Mercado Libre, Unilever.

---

## COLORS
- **Red** `#E8001D` — only accent, max 20% of any piece
- **Black** `#0A0A0A` — primary dark bg
- **White** `#F5F5F5` — primary light bg
- **Gray** `#6B6B6B` — secondary text
- Max 3 colors per piece. Red is never a full-page background.

**Contextual only (never as base):**
- `#003F8A` — incentive travel pieces
- `#1C2B3A` — evening galas
- `#C4873A` — award galas only (not a system color)

**Retired (never use):** parchment, espresso, gold as base, green.

---

## TYPOGRAPHY
| Role | Font | Style |
|------|------|-------|
| Display | **Anton** | UPPERCASE, -0.01em, lh 0.88, min 72px |
| Headline | **Oswald 700** | UPPERCASE, 0.02em, lh 1.05 |
| Body | **Open Sans 300** | 15–17px, lh 1.65 |
| Label | **DM Mono** | 8–10px, 0.20em, UPPERCASE |

Retired: Barlow Condensed, Cormorant Garamond, DM Sans.

---

## BORDER RADIUS
- `0` — photo overlays, red blocks
- `12px` — cards
- `20px` — large panels
- `999px` — all buttons (pill)

---

## GRAPHIC ELEMENTS
- **×** — brand signature. Use as superscript, section divider, watermark
- **Ghost numbers** — Anton stroke-only, opacity 0.04–0.06, as background layer
- **Eyebrow pattern** — `──── LABEL TEXT` in DM Mono red, always before section titles
- **Red accent line** — 1px, thin, never thick
- Diagonal lines max 1–2 per piece, 20–30° angle

---

## LAYOUT RULES
- Display type min 72px always
- Red area: 15–25% of total visual
- Radial blob gradients allowed in hero (opacity 0.06 max)
- Nav: backdrop-filter blur, bg rgba(10,10,10,0.9)
- Hover on cards: translateY(-4px) + box-shadow
- Buttons hover: translateY(-1px) + #B8001A

---

## PHOTOGRAPHY
- Always B&W base + red overlay 40–55% opacity
- Preferred: audiences, action, crowds, speakers
- Never: happy office stock, posed teamwork

---

## NEVER
- Soft gradients or pastel colors
- Rounded corners > 20px on content containers
- Gold as base color
- Green (retired brand color)
- Serif decorative fonts
- Border-radius on structural red blocks or photos
